Q1 Is the Educator App available for iOS or Windows?

No, it is just a web-app.

Q2 Can we record the videos using screencasting, video camera, pen tablet, mobile camera or other means and use that for uploading?

No, that will not be possible. The only way you can create educational courses on our platform is through Unacademy Educator App. There is no other way.

Q3 Will the educator be paid to make these courses?

No, We do not pay our educators for the courses. However, we have incentive structure for our educators based on the total views which they achieve on the platform, which enables them to cross the views milestones.
